# Porr Marci Disappeared

```
This is the story of a lovely lady,
caught up with a man who was shady.
Tall with red hair she may be ugly,
but at least she was not chubby.
Her son had been taken from her,
he simply disappeared in a blur.
Forced into theft to return him,
his future now seemed very grim.
When caught, she tried to speak out,
reunited with her son now I have no doubt.
```
